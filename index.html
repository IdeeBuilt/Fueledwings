Our Journey

From A Dream
To A Dining Room.

Brick by brick. Story by story. 🔥🧡

What started as a small dream slowly turned into something real. We began with simple deliveries — no shop, no big plans, just the passion to serve and the hope that one day, it would grow.

Then came the pop-up kiosk. It was small, barely enough space for everything, but it was our first brave step out into the world. Eventually, we managed to rent a small space — a place we could finally call our own. It wasn't perfect, but it was ours, and that made all the difference.

Things got tough along the way. At one point, we couldn't keep our main branch in Jaro going due to limited funds. But just when we thought we had reached a dead end, someone saw potential in what we built. They franchised the Jaro branch and gave it a second chance through renovation — a turning point we'll never forget.

Our very first franchise was born from belief and trust.

Though it eventually had to close, it opened doors we never imagined. Not long after, another opportunity came — a second franchise, a new branch. And this time, the momentum didn't stop.

Now, we're here with a full restaurant, a bigger team, and a community that has supported us from day one.

This journey wasn't smooth or perfect. There were delays, doubts, and closed doors. But every step taught us something. Every failure shaped us. Every small beginning had a purpose.

We're not just building a business. We're building a dream — brick by brick, story by story. Thank you for being part of this journey.
